# Store Listing Draft

## App Name

RuneGate Defense

## Short Description

Defend the crystal through 10 fantasy lane-defense stages.

## Long Description

RuneGate Defense is a compact 2D fantasy defense game for Android. Build a six-hero formation, defend the Kingdom Crystal across three lanes, choose rune cards between waves, earn gold, and upgrade your team as the Goblin Forest grows more dangerous.

## Key Features

- 3-lane defense battles
- 6 MVP heroes
- 6 monsters and 1 boss
- 20 rune cards
- 10-stage Goblin Forest progression
- Local JSON save
- Offline-first prototype structure

## Controls

- Heroes attack automatically.
- Tap skill buttons when cooldowns are ready.
- Choose one rune card after non-boss waves.
- Spend gold on permanent upgrades.

## Privacy And Monetization Draft

Current v1.0 candidate:

- No login
